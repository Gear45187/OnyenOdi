WELCOME MESSAGE FROM THE PRESIDENT'S DESK

Fellow Alumni, Esteemed Members, and Distinguished Guests, I, Stella Onyendinazu, President of the ACSS Class of 94, am honored to welcome you to our newly developed website. This platform represents a significant milestone in our journey as a united and vibrant alumni community. As we reflect on our humble beginnings, we recall the seeds of unity and cooperation sown in 2021 under the visionary leadership of Hon. Wale Martins. We remember the registration of our association with CAC, the drafting and harmonization of our constitution, and the successful organization of our first reunion party in 2022. This website serves as a testament to our growth, resilience, and dedication to our shared values. It provides a platform for us to reconnect, share our experiences, and forge new paths together. As we embark on this new chapter, I invite you to explore our website, engage with our community, and contribute to our ongoing journey. Let us continue to uphold the spirit of ACSS Class of 94, fostering a culture of excellence, empathy, and unity. Thank you for your unwavering support, and I look forward to connecting with you all on this exciting new platform. Together, we will achieve greatness!

Stella Onyendinazu

President,
ACSS Class of 94
