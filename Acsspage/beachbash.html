BEACH BASH

UNFORGETTABLE REUNION: Class of '94 set rocks artican beach and Panache Exclusive Lounge

"The Class of '94 Set is making waves! Our recent reunion was a two-day extravaganza that left us all grinning from ear to ear. It all kicked off on Friday, May 2nd, at the swanky Panache Exclusive Lounge in Oregun, where we gathered for a Karaoke Night that will go down in history! Our President and other members brought the house down with their soulful voices and electrifying performances. The night was filled with laughter, music, and memories that will last a lifetime.







The excitement continued on Saturday, May 3rd, as we descended upon the sun-kissed shores of Artican Beach in Ajah for our Beach Blast. The fun was non-stop, with thrilling games of Ludo, Money Play that kept us on the edge of our seats, and a feast of delicious food and refreshing drinks that fueled our merriment.

Our first class empowerment to Aloba Ebenezer

But what truly warmed our hearts was the spirit of giving and empowerment that defined our gathering. In a heartwarming display of solidarity and support, we came together to empower one of our own, presenting a gift of #600,000 to help furnish his business. It was a moment that showcased the true essence of our friendship and the Class of '94 Set's commitment to lifting each other up. As we soaked up the sun, sand, and sea breeze, we rediscovered the magic of our friendship and the joy of sharing moments together. It was a truly unforgettable experience, and we're grateful to everyone who made it possible. *A huge thank you to our amazing Class of '94 Set* for making this reunion one for the books! We're already counting down to the next one!
